CONSTRAINT TENSION
  Middleware is sync; SessionStore is async. We have three options:

  A. Make the middleware async. This is correct but breaks the implicit
     Express contract for any consumer that wraps it without await.
     Express itself handles async middleware fine since 4.x, but I want
     you to confirm there's no consumer that introspects the function
     arity (some libraries do).

  B. Keep the middleware sync; introduce a synchronous local cache that
     SessionStore writes through. Middleware reads from the local cache,
     SessionStore is the source of truth. Adds complexity but preserves
     the contract.

  C. Change scope: deprecate this middleware entirely, replace with a
     new async-aware one in src/middleware/auth-v2.ts. Migrate callers
     opt-in over time.
